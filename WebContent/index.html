High Speed Encryption-Decryption

Our public-key encryption decryption algorithm is super-fast and extremely portable. Circulate one public key across your organisation and youre done for the day.

Data-analysis

We provide basic data analysis features for your files and databases as well. So that is one mildly less thing to worry about.

Data Integrity checker

We have our own algorithm to check your file/database integrity. So if someone is tampering with them or making the data go haywire, you'll know.
